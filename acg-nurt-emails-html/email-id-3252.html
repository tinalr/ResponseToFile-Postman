Drive Team Goals with Learning Paths
 
 

With your Linux Academy for Business subscription, you have access to a wide array of Learning Paths, which help guide members of your organization through a specific track. You can choose from an existing Learning Path or create your own, depending on your needs!

Tip: Create an onboarding “Intro to Linux Academy” Learning Path that you can assign members to as soon as they’ve accepted their LA invitation! This could include introductory courses for your cloud provider, an overview of Linux tools, and a first certification course.
