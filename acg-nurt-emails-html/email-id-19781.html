Get started on your journey to becoming a Cloud Guru!
 
 
 

WEEK 1

Welcome to the A Cloud Guru Accelerator Program for Google Certified Associate Cloud Engineer. Congratulations on taking the first steps towards preparing for your certification! We understand it can feel a little overwhelming to study for an important exam – don’t worry, we’ve got you covered.

You’re about to embark on a seven week journey to build the skills and confidence that you need to pass your exam with flying colors! Here’s how this will work:

This program uses a Watch, Do, Read, and Explore framework – each week, you’ll receive a lesson plan from us, including which lessons to watch, which labs and assessments to do, and which resources to read. Depending on the week, we’ll sprinkle in the explore option, providing you with additional resources directly from Google Cloud! For optimal success, we recommend investing 3-4 hours per week on this program.

Let’s get started, shall we? This week’s lessons are all about building your foundation, including an overview of Google Cloud Platform (GCP) products and services and how they fit together as key cloud building blocks. We’ll also help familiarize you with the exam structure so you know what to expect!
