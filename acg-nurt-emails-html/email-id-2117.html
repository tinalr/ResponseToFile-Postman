Why Central Cloud Teams Fail
 
 

(And How to Save Yours)
 
 

By setting your team up as the cloud gatekeepers for your organization, you may create a bottleneck for the entire company’s cloud adoption efforts - the exact opposite of your goal.

No one can survive long-term as the sole repository of cloud knowledge for their entire organization. Save your central cloud team’s sanity and speed up cloud adoption through comprehensive fluency.

Your cloud center of excellence will die unless it can figure out how to reproduce, by changing your mission from expertise (knowing the most about cloud) to enablement (creating more people who know about cloud).
