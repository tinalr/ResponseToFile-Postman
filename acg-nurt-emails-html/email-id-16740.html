We hope you’re getting acclimated to your dashboard and have had a chance to explore the cloud training library (maybe you’ve even started a course, you overachiever).

In addition to courses, one of our favorite ways to get hands-on with cloud learning is the Cloud Playground!
 
 
 
 

Our Cloud Playground offers three risk-free features:
 
 
  1. Instant Terminal: No extensions, no add-ons, or extra packages needed! Connect to the ACG terminal with any web browser, without using port 22 on your local machine - plus no firewall restrictions!
  2. Cloud Servers: Launch up to 9 virtual machines, risk-free with Cloud Servers! Set up pre-configured, auto-provisioned servers in just a few clicks.
  3. Cloud Sandbox: Spin up a secure Cloud Sandbox in no time! Get your hands cloudy with AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure and we’ll take care of managing and securing your temporary sandbox.
