5 reasons employers should invest in continuous IT training
 
 

Companies should invest in their employees. Seems obvious enough, but when it comes down to the nitty gritty of allocating budget, we too often see that providing opportunities for continuous training or education slip off the priority list.

The truth is, improving proficiencies benefits employers as much as employees, especially in the IT industry where the innovation never stops. A study by Robert Half showed that a strong learning culture at work led to 30-50% higher retention rates.

As a company, you'll sharpen your competitive edge, save on time and costs with more highly skilled employees, and boost retention with happier employees who feel valued.
