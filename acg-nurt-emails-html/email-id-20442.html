Week 1
 
 

Welcome to the A Cloud Guru Accelerator Program for AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate (SAA-C03). Congratulations on taking the first steps towards preparing for your certification! We understand it can feel a little overwhelming to study for an important exam – don’t worry, we’ve got you covered.

You’re about to embark on a fourteen week journey to build the skills and confidence that you need to pass your exam with flying colors! Here’s how this will work:

This program uses a Watch, Do, and Read framework – each week, you’ll receive a lesson plan from us, including which lessons to watch, which labs and assessments to do, and which resources to read. We’ll also provide reading materials directly from Amazon. We highly recommend reviewing these, as they’ll be key in your success during the exam. For optimal success, we recommend investing 3-4 hours per week on this program.

Let’s get started, shall we? Week 1 is all about building a foundation. We’ll introduce you to the course and your AWS Training Architect. You’ll learn about the fundamentals of AWS, including Identity and Access Management (IAM). We’ll wrap up with a few assessments and Hands-on Labs to test your skills and get you familiar with AWS as a whole.
