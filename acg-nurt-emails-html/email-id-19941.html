Week 1
 
 

Welcome to the A Cloud Guru Accelerator Program for AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner. Congratulations on taking the first steps towards preparing for your certification! We understand it can feel a little overwhelming to study for an important exam – don’t worry, we’ve got you covered.

You’re about to embark on an eight week journey to build the skills and confidence that you need to pass your exam with flying colors! If you are brand new to the cloud, we recommend checking out Cloud Computing Foundations to gain a foundational knowledge in less than 30 minutes!

This program uses a Watch, Do, and Read framework – each week, you’ll receive a lesson plan from us, including which lessons to watch, which labs and assessments to do, and which resources to read. For optimal success, we recommend investing 3-4 hours per week on this program.

Let’s get started, shall we? This week, it’s all about building a foundation. You’ll first get a glimpse of what to expect on the Certified Cloud Practitioner certification exam. Then, we’ll dive right into the advantages of cloud computing and get familiar with your AWS account.
